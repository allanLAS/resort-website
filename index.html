East Africa's Best Island Resort

Are you tired of the hustle and bustle of your daily life? Take a break and come visit us! Boasting an outdoor swimming pool and tennis court, Montana is set in Watamu, 1.6km from the main road

We take pride in ensuring our guests enjoy the most relaxing and comfortable stay in fully furnished rooms and suites offering spectacular ocean, pool and garden views. Breakfast is also available every morning and includes continental and vegeteraian options
